SoccerVis

This project aims to demonstrate the power and utility of D3js (Data Driven Documents)

This robust javascript library is the perfect tool for data visualisation. Our project aims to provide a means of comparing the statistics of various soccer players over the course of 19 games.

This provides a means of tracking the performance of a player on a continuing basis.
